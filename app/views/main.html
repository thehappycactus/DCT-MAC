What Is the MAC?

The MPC (Most Placeable Candidate) Availability Chart - or "MAC" - is a list of 3 candidates of ours currently on the market for each of the top 10 Priority Roles on the market today. They range in seniority from Junior to Senior.

How Do I Use the MAC?

You can click on any name on the MAC to see a short summary of the candidate's background - their current title, highlights of their skillset, and a fun fact. If you see a candidate you're interested in, click their "Learn More!" button to contact us for further information.

I Don't See the Role I'm Searching For!

If you like the candidates, but don't see the specific role you want to fill, you can contact us here and we'd be more than happy to help you with your search!
